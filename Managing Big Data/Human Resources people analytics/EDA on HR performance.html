The "PerfScoreID" ranges from 1 to 4.
PerfScoreID = 1 --------- PerformanceScore = "PIP" (performance improvement plan, the worst situation)
PerfScoreID = 2 --------- PerformanceScore = "Needs Improvement"
PerfScoreID = 3 --------- PerformanceScore = "Fully Meets"
PerfScoreID = 4 --------- PerformanceScore = "Exceeds"

From the stacked bar chart, we can find out that:

  1. Most employees have a "fully meets" performance.
  2. The proportion and the number of employees with "exceeds" performance are high under manager 2 and 12.
  3. Manager 12 also has many employees who are under PIP.
  4. Manager 4, 14, 18, 19, 30 and 40 lead a group well. Their employees all have a performance score 3. In the data preprocessing, 40 represents null manager IDs, but these unknown managers have a group of nice employees.
